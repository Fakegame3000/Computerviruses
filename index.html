Overview

Computer viruses are malicious programs that can infect systems, destroy data, and disrupt businesses. For companies, these attacks pose serious ethical and operational risks ‚Äî leading to stolen information, financial loss, and a damaged reputation.

Risks & Potential Problems

  • Financial Loss: Companies may lose millions in recovery and downtime.
  • Data Breaches: Sensitive information can be stolen and misused.
  • Reputation Damage: Customers lose trust after security failures.
  • Legal Consequences: Businesses can be sued for not protecting user data.

Tips for Parents, Students, and Entrepreneurs

  • Install trusted antivirus software on all devices.
  • Teach students and employees to avoid suspicious links and emails.
  • Update operating systems and apps regularly to patch vulnerabilities.
  • Back up important files using cloud services or external drives.
  • Entrepreneurs: You can launch ethical startups focused on cybersecurity, education, and safer digital tools.
